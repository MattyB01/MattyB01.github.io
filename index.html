Welcome to the Austral-Asian Community Church


AACC exists to bring the Kingdom of God to the world by Reaching, Touching and Changing Lives in our relevant spheres of influence in Adelaide, Australia and beyond. The Austral-Asian Community Church (AACC) is an interdenominational and intergenerational church, the oldest multicultural church in Adelaide.


Located in the suburb of Tusmore, the congregation today comprises members of diverse cultures from all over Adelaide.
